mitigate

缓解；减轻（风险 / 损害）

/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ Academic

meaning · 释义

to make something less severe or painful

使某事变得不那么严重 / 让痛苦或损害变小

examples · 例句

01"These measures aim to mitigate the long-term impact on biodiversity."

这些措施的目的是减轻对生物多样性的长期影响。

02"The company took steps to mitigate the financial damage from the recall."

公司采取措施来缓解召回事件带来的经济损失。

03"Early detection can significantly mitigate the spread of the disease."

早期发现可以极大地减缓疾病的扩散。
